Ramadan Tracker

A simple, distraction-free way to track your daily Ramadan worship and build consistent habits throughout the blessed month.

Features

  • Track fasting, prayers, Quran reading, and custom goals — all in one place.
  • Works completely offline. Your data stays on your device, never leaves it.
  • Clean, focused interface with no ads or distractions.

FAQ

Do I need to create an account?

No. The app works instantly in your browser — no sign-up, no login.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your data is stored locally on your device. We never collect or see it.

Does it work without internet?

Yes. Once loaded, the app works fully offline as a PWA.
