Umrah & Hajj Guide

A clear, step-by-step companion for your pilgrimage — duas, rituals, and practical guidance, all in one place.

Features

  • Step-by-step walkthrough of every Umrah and Hajj ritual with authentic duas.
  • Works fully offline — perfect for when you're at the Haram with no signal.
  • No account needed, no ads, no data collection. Just guidance.

FAQ

Can I use this during my pilgrimage?

Absolutely. It's designed to work offline — load it before you travel and use it anywhere.

Does it cover both Umrah and Hajj?

Yes. The guide includes rituals and duas for both Umrah and Hajj.

Is it free?

Completely free. This is a nonprofit project — no subscriptions, no in-app purchases.
